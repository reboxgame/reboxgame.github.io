3. Automatic Renewal Policy

To ensure seamless service, your subscription is set to automatically renew. The renewal fee will be charged to your Apple ID account within 24 hours prior to the end of the current billing cycle. The cost of renewal will match your initial subscription price unless otherwise notified.

4. Subscription Control

You maintain full control over your subscription. You may disable auto-renewal at any time via your Apple ID settings. To avoid being charged for the next cycle, auto-renewal must be turned off at least 24 hours before the current period expires.

How to Cancel:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iOS device.
  2. Tap on your Apple ID (your name at the top).
  3. Select Subscriptions.
  4. Choose Watch Games from the list.
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription.

Note: Cancellations become effective at the end of the current billing period.
